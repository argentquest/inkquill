# Twitter/X Platform Guide for inkAndQuill

## Sample Templates Available
- **Thread Introduction Template**: Welcome_Thread_Introduction.html / .txt
- **Direct Single Tweet Template**: Welcome_Single_Tweet_Direct.html / .txt

## Platform Overview
- **Primary Audience**: Real-time engagement seekers, professionals, news consumers, writers
- **Content Type**: Text tweets, threads, images, videos, spaces
- **Optimal Post Times**: 9 AM - 10 AM, 7 PM - 9 PM (EST)
- **Character Limits**: 280 characters per tweet
- **Hashtag Strategy**: 2-3 hashtags maximum per tweet

## Content Strategy
- **Tweet Types**: Tips, quotes, threads, polls, retweets with comments
- **Thread Strategy**: Educational content, step-by-step guides, stories
- **Engagement**: Quick responses, community participation
- **Trending**: Participate in relevant writing/AI hashtags

## Brand Guidelines
- **Voice**: Conversational, helpful, encouraging
- **Tone**: Professional but approachable
- **Response Time**: Within 2-4 hours during business hours
- **Content Mix**: 70% valuable content, 30% promotional

## Engagement Tactics
- **Twitter Chats**: Participate in #WritingCommunity chats
- **Polls**: Ask audience questions about writing preferences
- **Threads**: Share detailed tips and tutorials
- **Retweets**: Share and comment on relevant writing content

## Content Calendar Suggestions
- **Daily**: Motivational writing quotes or tips
- **Monday**: #MondayMotivation writing inspiration
- **Tuesday**: #TipTuesday platform features or writing advice
- **Wednesday**: #WritingCommunity engagement
- **Thursday**: Throwback to classic writing advice
- **Friday**: #FeatureFriday community spotlights
- **Weekend**: Writing prompts and creative exercises

## Key Metrics to Track
- Tweet impressions and engagement rate
- Profile visits and follower growth
- Click-through rate to website
- Hashtag performance
- Thread completion rate
- Mentions and brand sentiment
