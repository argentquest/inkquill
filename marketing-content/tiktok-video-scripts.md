# TikTok Video Scripts for AI Storytelling Platform

## TikTok Strategy Overview
- **Target Audience**: Gen Z and Millennial writers, aspiring authors, creative professionals
- **Content Style**: Quick tips, behind-the-scenes, trending formats, educational, entertaining
- **Video Length**: 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30-45 seconds)
- **Posting Frequency**: 1-2 videos per day
- **Best Times**: 6-10 AM, 7-9 PM, 9-12 AM

## Script 1: Writing Tip Hook
**Title**: "POV: You're trying to write dialogue but it sounds like robots talking"

**Hook (0-3s)**: 
*[Text overlay: "Your dialogue before this tip"]*

**Visual Direction**: Split screen showing stiff vs natural dialogue, use trending transition sound
**Trending Audio**: Use popular "before/after" sound or trending dialogue audio

## TikTok Best Practices Summary

### Video Structure:
- **Hook (0-3s)**: Grab attention immediately
- **Content (3-25s)**: Deliver value quickly
- **CTA (25-30s)**: Engage audience

### Visual Guidelines:
- **Lighting**: Bright, natural light preferred
- **Framing**: Vertical format, face clearly visible
- **Text**: Large, readable fonts with high contrast
- **Props**: Simple, relevant, eye-catching

### Audio Strategy:
- **Trending Sounds**: Use current trending audio
- **Original Audio**: Create unique sounds for series
- **Music**: Match energy to content
- **Voiceover**: Clear, enthusiastic delivery

### Hashtag Strategy:
- **Primary**: #AuthorTok #WritingTips #BookTok
- **Secondary**: #WritingCommunity #CreativeWriting #WritingAdvice
- **Specific**: Match content (e.g., #CharacterDevelopment)
- **Trending**: Research and include 1-2 trending tags

### Engagement Tactics:
- **Comments**: Respond within 1 hour
- **Duets**: Encourage and participate in duets
- **Stitches**: Create response videos to comments
- **Live**: Host weekly writing Q&As
- **Series**: Create recurring content themes

### Content Calendar:
- **Monday**: Motivation/Mindset
- **Tuesday**: Tips/Tutorials  
- **Wednesday**: Writing Hacks
- **Thursday**: Community/Engagement
- **Friday**: Fun/Entertainment
- **Weekend**: Behind-the-scenes/Personal

### Performance Metrics:
- **Completion Rate**: Aim for 70%+
- **Engagement Rate**: Target 5-10%
- **Shares**: Create shareable, valuable content
- **Comments**: Ask questions to drive discussion
- **Follows**: Include clear value proposition
