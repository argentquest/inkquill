Beginner Understanding Your World

The World Detail page is your command center for building rich, interconnected universes. Here you can manage all aspects of your world and see how everything connects.

World Information

Basic details like name, description, and privacy settings. This is what others see when you share your world.

Element Counts

Quick stats showing how many characters, locations, lore items, and stories you've created.

Recent Activity

Timeline of recent changes and additions to help you track your progress.

Quick Actions

Fast access to common tasks like adding new elements or starting stories.

Navigation Overview

From the World Detail page, you can access:

  • World Hierarchy: Visual tree view of all world elements
  • Element Lists: Dedicated pages for characters, locations, and lore
  • Story Manager: View and edit stories set in this world
  • World Map: Visual representation of locations and connections

Exploring Your World

5 minutes
  1. 1

  2. 2
    Check element counts to see what you've created
  3. 3
    Use navigation buttons to explore different sections
  4. 4
    Review recent activity to see your progress

Intermediate Editing World Details

Basic Information Editing ▼

Click the "Edit World" button to modify:

  • Name: The title of your world
  • Description: Summary of your world's concept
  • Privacy Settings: Who can view your world
  • Tags: Categories for better organization
💡
Tip: A good world description helps others understand your creative vision and helps you stay focused on your core concept.
Privacy Controls ▼
Setting Who Can View Best For
Private Only you Personal projects, early development
Shared Invited users Collaboration with friends or co-writers
Public Everyone Showcasing work, community building
Collaboration Features ▼

When sharing worlds, you can:

  • Invite specific users as collaborators
  • Set different permission levels (view, comment, edit)
  • Track changes made by different contributors
  • Set up notification preferences for updates

World Statistics

The statistics panel shows:

  • Creation Date: When you first created this world
  • Last Modified: Most recent update to any element
  • Total Elements: Combined count of all world components
  • Word Count: Total text across all descriptions
  • Completeness Score: How well-developed your world is

Advanced AI-Powered World Building

🧠 Smart Content Generation

AI can help expand your world by generating detailed descriptions, suggesting connections between elements, and creating rich backstories.

World Consistency Checking ▼

AI analyzes your world for:

  • Contradictions: Conflicting information between elements
  • Plot Holes: Gaps in logic or continuity
  • Missing Connections: Elements that should be related but aren't
  • Tone Consistency: Ensuring all content matches your world's mood
💰 Cost: 5-15 credits per consistency check
Automated Suggestions ▼

AI can suggest:

  • New Characters: Based on your existing cast and story needs
  • Location Details: Expand basic locations with rich descriptions
  • Cultural Elements: Add depth with customs, languages, and traditions
  • Story Hooks: Plot ideas that fit naturally in your world
✨
Best Practice: Use AI suggestions as inspiration, not replacement for your creativity. Always review and personalize AI-generated content.
RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) ▼

RAG technology allows AI to:

  • Access and reference your existing world content
  • Maintain consistency with established lore
  • Suggest connections based on similar themes
  • Generate content that fits your world's tone
When you add new content, AI automatically:
1. Embeds the text into searchable vectors
2. Indexes it for future retrieval
3. Creates connections with existing elements
4. Updates the knowledge graph

Cost Management

To manage AI costs effectively:

  • Batch Operations: Process multiple elements at once
  • Selective Generation: Only use AI for complex or creative tasks
  • Review Before Processing: Ensure requests are clear and specific
  • Monitor Usage: Check credit balance regularly

Intermediate Organizing Your World

Tags and Categories ▼

Use tags to organize elements by:

  • Story Arc: Group elements by narrative importance
  • Geography: Organize by regions or continents
  • Time Period: Separate historical from contemporary elements
  • Completion Status: Track which elements need more work
Relationships and Connections ▼

The association system lets you define:

  • Character Relationships: Family, friends, enemies, mentors
  • Location Hierarchies: Cities within regions, rooms within buildings
  • Story Connections: Which elements appear in which stories
  • Lore Dependencies: How different pieces of knowledge connect

Creating Meaningful Connections

10 minutes
  1. 1
    Select an element you want to connect
  2. 2
    Click "Manage Associations" from the context menu
  3. 3
    Choose the type of connection and target element
  4. 4
    Define roles and add descriptive notes
  5. 5
    Save to create a bidirectional relationship
Backup and Version Control ▼

Protect your work with:

  • Automatic Backups: Daily snapshots of your world state
  • Version History: Track changes to individual elements
  • Export Options: Download your world data in various formats
  • Import Tools: Restore from backups or merge with other worlds
