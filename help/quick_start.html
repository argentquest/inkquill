🚀 Quick Start Guide

Your first story in 3 simple steps

Welcome! This guide will get you creating AI-assisted stories in just 3 steps. The entire process takes about 5-10 minutes.

Step 1: Create Your World (e.g., Wonderland)

Your World is the AI's source of truth. Start by going to "My Worlds" and choosing "Import from Book". When prompted, enter the title: Alice's Adventures in Wonderland. The AI will populate a new world for you.

Your new World will contain:

Characters: Alice, The White Rabbit, The Mad Hatter Locations: The Rabbit-Hole, The Mad Tea-Party Lore: "Eat Me" cakes, "Drink Me" potions
Go to "My Worlds"

Step 2: Start a Story & Link It

Now that you have Wonderland, create a new narrative. Go to "My Stories" and click "Create New Story". The most important step is to select your "Wonderland" world from the dropdown menu to tell the AI which universe to use.

Tip: You could name your story "The Cheshire Cat's Riddle" to fit the theme
Start a New Story

Step 3: Write with an AI That Knows Wonderland

This is where world-building pays off. Inside your story, create an Act and enter the editor. Now, when you ask the AI to write about the White Rabbit, it will know he has a pocket watch. It's writing within the context of your world.

Tip: Link specific characters like Alice to your story for even smarter AI suggestions
Go to Your Stories
Detailed Instructions: Follow these step-by-step instructions for a more thorough walkthrough.

Step 1: World Creation - Detailed

  1. Navigate to Worlds: Click "My Worlds" in the sidebar navigation
  2. Choose Import Method: Click "Import from Book" button
  3. Enter Book Title: Type Alice's Adventures in Wonderland in the text field
  4. Wait for Processing: The AI will analyze the book and extract world elements (2-3 minutes)
  5. Review Results: Check the generated characters, locations, and lore items
  6. Customize (Optional): Edit any generated content to match your vision
⏱️ Estimated time: 5-10 minutes 💡 Alternative: Use the World Builder Wizard for original worlds

Step 2: Story Creation - Detailed

  1. Navigate to Stories: Click "My Stories" in the sidebar
  2. Start Creation: Click "Create New Story" button
  3. Fill Basic Info: Enter story title, description, and target audience
  4. Link Your World: Most Important! Select "Wonderland" from the world dropdown
  5. Choose Story Class: Select appropriate genre (Fantasy, Adventure, etc.)
  6. Create Initial Structure: Add your first Act with a descriptive name
⏱️ Estimated time: 3-5 minutes 🎯 Key: Linking the world enables AI context

Step 3: AI Writing - Detailed

  1. Enter Act Editor: Click on your Act to open the writing interface
  2. Start Writing: Begin typing your story opening
  3. Engage AI Assistant: Use the AI panel to ask for help or continuation
  4. Reference World Elements: Mention characters or locations - AI knows them!
  5. Use Contextual Suggestions: AI will offer relevant plot developments
  6. Iterate and Refine: Work with AI to develop your narrative
✨ AI knows your world's characters, locations, and lore 🔄 Real-time collaboration with AI assistance
Common Issues: Solutions to frequently encountered problems during quick start.

World Import Issues

Problem: "Import from Book" doesn't work or takes too long

Solutions:

  • Try a different public domain book title
  • Use "World Builder Wizard" instead for AI-guided creation
  • Create a manual world and add elements one by one
  • Check your internet connection and try again

Story-World Linking Issues

Problem: AI doesn't seem to know about my world elements

Solutions:

  • Verify the world is selected in the story settings
  • Check that characters/locations exist in your world
  • Wait a moment for the AI context to load
  • Explicitly mention character names in your writing

AI Assistant Issues

Problem: AI responses are generic or unhelpful

Solutions:

  • Be more specific in your prompts
  • Mention specific characters or locations from your world
  • Use the world chat feature to discuss ideas first
  • Check your credit balance - low credits affect AI quality

Pro Tips for Success

  • Start Small: Begin with a simple story idea before attempting complex narratives
  • Rich Worlds = Better AI: The more detailed your world, the smarter the AI assistance
  • Use Document Manager: Upload reference materials for additional AI context
  • Experiment with Prompts: Try different ways of asking the AI for help
  • Save Frequently: Use Ctrl+S or rely on auto-save in the editors
  • Join the Community: Visit the forum for tips and inspiration from other writers
  • Monitor Credits: Keep track of AI usage in your billing dashboard
