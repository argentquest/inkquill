# Facebook Platform Guide for inkAndQuill

## Sample Templates Available
- **Detailed Introduction Template**: Welcome_Post_Detailed.html / .txt

## Platform Overview
- **Primary Audience**: Diverse age groups, community-focused, family-oriented
- **Content Type**: Text posts, images, videos, live streams, events, groups
- **Optimal Post Times**: 1 PM - 3 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM (EST)
- **Character Limits**: 63,206 characters (but shorter is better for engagement)
- **Algorithm**: Favors engagement, meaningful conversations, video content

## Content Strategy
- **Post Types**: Community updates, educational content, user spotlights, tips
- **Group Strategy**: Create private groups for writers and educators
- **Event Strategy**: Host virtual writing workshops and Q&As
- **Community Focus**: Encourage discussions and user-generated content

## Brand Guidelines
- **Voice**: Warm, inclusive, community-focused
- **Tone**: Educational yet friendly, supportive
- **Visual Style**: Consistent with brand colors, high-quality images
- **Community Management**: Active engagement, prompt responses

## Engagement Tactics
- **Comments**: Ask questions to encourage responses
- **User Generated Content**: Share community success stories
- **Facebook Groups**: Create dedicated spaces for different audiences
- **Live Videos**: Regular Q&A sessions and tutorials

## Content Calendar Suggestions
- **Monday**: Motivational Monday posts
- **Tuesday**: Tutorial Tuesday (how-to content)
- **Wednesday**: Community Spotlight Wednesday
- **Thursday**: Tips Thursday (writing advice)
- **Friday**: Fun Friday (writing prompts, challenges)
- **Weekend**: Community discussions and engagement

## Key Metrics to Track
- Post reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Page likes and follows
- Website clicks
- Video views and completion rates
- Group member growth and activity
