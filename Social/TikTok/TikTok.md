# TikTok Platform Guide for inkAndQuill

## Sample Templates Available
- **Quick & Energetic Script Template**: Welcome_Video_Quick_Energetic.html / .txt

## Platform Overview
- **Primary Audience**: Gen Z and young millennials (16-34), creative content consumers
- **Content Type**: Short-form vertical videos (15-60 seconds)
- **Optimal Post Times**: 6 AM - 10 AM, 7 PM - 9 PM (EST)
- **Video Length**: 15-30 seconds for highest engagement
- **Algorithm**: Favors completion rate, engagement, trending sounds

## Content Strategy
- **Hook Strategy**: Capture attention in first 3 seconds
- **Trending Audio**: Use popular sounds and music
- **Quick Tips**: Bite-sized writing advice and hacks
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Show app features and writing process

## Brand Guidelines
- **Voice**: Energetic, relatable, fun but informative
- **Tone**: Casual, enthusiastic, trend-aware
- **Visual Style**: High-energy, colorful, on-brand
- **Captions**: Minimal text overlay, engaging hooks

## Engagement Tactics
- **Duets**: Respond to user questions and content
- **Challenges**: Create writing-related hashtag challenges
- **Trends**: Participate in relevant trending topics
- **Comments**: Quick, engaging responses

## Content Calendar Suggestions
- **Daily**: Writing tips, motivational content, app features
- **Trending**: Participate in relevant challenges and trends
- **Weekly**: Behind-the-scenes content, user spotlights
- **Educational**: Quick tutorials on writing techniques

## Key Metrics to Track
- Video views and completion rate
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Follower growth
- Hashtag performance
- Profile visits
- Website clicks from bio
