# Instagram Platform Guide for inkAndQuill

## Sample Templates Available
- **Visual Focus Template**: Welcome_Post_Visual_Focus.html / .txt
- **Community Focus Template**: Welcome_Post_Community_Focus.html / .txt

## Platform Overview
- **Primary Audience**: Visual content consumers, creative professionals, younger demographics (18-34)
- **Content Type**: Images, Stories, Reels, IGTV
- **Optimal Post Times**: 11 AM - 1 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM (EST)
- **Character Limits**: 2,200 characters for captions
- **Hashtag Strategy**: 20-30 hashtags, mix of popular and niche

## Content Strategy
- **Visual Focus**: High-quality images with consistent branding
- **Story Features**: Behind-the-scenes content, polls, Q&As
- **Reels Strategy**: Quick tips, transformations, trending audio
- **IGTV**: Longer tutorials and educational content

## Brand Guidelines
- **Colors**: Blue-teal gradient matching logo
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts
- **Image Style**: Professional yet approachable
- **Logo Usage**: Visible but not overwhelming

## Engagement Tactics
- **Stories**: Interactive stickers, polls, questions
- **User-Generated Content**: Repost community stories
- **Hashtag Campaigns**: #inkAndQuillStory, #AIWritingJourney
- **Collaborations**: Partner with writing influencers

## Content Calendar Suggestions
- **Monday**: Motivational writing quotes
- **Tuesday**: Tutorial Tuesday (platform features)
- **Wednesday**: World-building Wednesday (showcase user worlds)
- **Thursday**: Tips Thursday (writing advice)
- **Friday**: Feature Friday (community spotlights)
- **Saturday**: Story Saturday (user success stories)
- **Sunday**: Sunday Inspiration (creative prompts)

## Key Metrics to Track
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate
- Story completion rate
- Profile visits
- Website clicks
- Hashtag performance
