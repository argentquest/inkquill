# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification outlines the requirements for implementing a comprehensive marketing plan for our AI-powered storytelling platform. The marketing plan will focus on multi-channel digital marketing strategies, social media presence, content marketing, community building, and user acquisition. The implementation will include both strategic planning documents and technical components to support marketing operations.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a marketing manager, I want a comprehensive social media strategy document, so that I can effectively promote the platform across multiple social networks with consistent messaging and optimal content scheduling.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN creating social media strategy THEN the system SHALL provide detailed platform-specific strategies for Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, Reddit, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and YouTube
2. WHEN defining content strategy THEN the system SHALL specify content types, posting frequencies, hashtag strategies, and engagement tactics for each platform
3. WHEN planning content calendar THEN the system SHALL provide 90-day content calendar templates with platform-specific content suggestions
4. IF targeting different audience segments THEN the system SHALL customize messaging and content approach for aspiring writers, hobbyist storytellers, professional authors, content creators, and educators

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a content marketing specialist, I want a structured content marketing framework with comprehensive SEO strategy, so that I can create valuable content that drives organic traffic and showcases platform capabilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN developing content strategy THEN the system SHALL define content pillars including writing craft, AI assistance, worldbuilding guides, user success stories, and industry insights
2. WHEN planning blog content THEN the system SHALL provide editorial calendar with 2 weekly in-depth articles, bi-weekly success stories, and weekly AI tips
3. WHEN conducting keyword research THEN the system SHALL identify primary keywords (AI writing assistant, worldbuilding software, story generator), secondary keywords (creative writing tools, character development software), and 100+ long-tail keywords
4. WHEN optimizing content THEN the system SHALL provide SEO-optimized article templates, meta descriptions, title tags, and internal linking strategies
5. WHEN creating lead magnets THEN the system SHALL provide templates for worldbuilding guides, character worksheets, and AI prompt collections optimized for search discovery

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a community manager, I want community building strategies and tools, so that I can foster an engaged community of writers and storytellers around the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN building community presence THEN the system SHALL provide Discord server structure with channels for writing challenges, feedback, and AI tips
2. WHEN planning virtual events THEN the system SHALL schedule monthly workshops, quarterly masterclasses, and weekly writing sprints
3. WHEN encouraging user-generated content THEN the system SHALL implement story showcase programs and community voting systems
4. WHEN managing community engagement THEN the system SHALL provide moderation guidelines and engagement protocols

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a digital marketing manager, I want email marketing automation and lead nurturing systems, so that I can convert prospects into active users through targeted communication sequences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN setting up email sequences THEN the system SHALL provide welcome series, nurture campaigns, and re-engagement sequences
2. WHEN segmenting audiences THEN the system SHALL categorize by user type, activity level, and feature usage
3. WHEN creating lead magnets THEN the system SHALL offer valuable resources like writing templates and AI guides
4. WHEN tracking performance THEN the system SHALL monitor open rates, click-through rates, and conversion metrics

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a bootstrap marketing manager, I want zero-budget organic growth strategies, so that I can acquire new users through free marketing channels and community-driven growth tactics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN implementing organic social media THEN the system SHALL focus on authentic engagement, user-generated content, and viral content strategies without paid promotion
2. WHEN building SEO presence THEN the system SHALL prioritize high-value organic keywords, guest posting opportunities, and backlink building through partnerships
3. WHEN leveraging free tools THEN the system SHALL utilize free versions of marketing tools, open-source alternatives, and platform-native features
4. WHEN measuring organic growth THEN the system SHALL track organic traffic, social media reach, referral traffic, and word-of-mouth metrics

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As an influencer marketing coordinator, I want an influencer outreach and management system, so that I can partner with writing coaches, authors, and content creators to authentically promote the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN identifying influencers THEN the system SHALL categorize writing coaches, published authors, and BookTok/Bookstagram creators
2. WHEN managing campaigns THEN the system SHALL provide onboarding guides, tracking systems, and performance metrics
3. WHEN measuring success THEN the system SHALL track engagement, signups, and retention from each influencer partnership
4. WHEN scaling outreach THEN the system SHALL provide templates and automation for influencer communication

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a marketing analytics manager, I want comprehensive tracking and measurement systems using Google Analytics and other tools, so that I can monitor marketing performance and optimize campaigns based on data-driven insights.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN setting up analytics THEN the system SHALL implement Google Analytics 4 with enhanced ecommerce tracking, conversion goals, and custom events
2. WHEN tracking acquisition THEN the system SHALL measure cost per acquisition, channel attribution, and conversion rates across all marketing channels
3. WHEN monitoring engagement THEN the system SHALL track social media engagement, email performance, blog metrics, and user behavior flows
4. WHEN measuring retention THEN the system SHALL monitor user retention at 30/60/90 days, feature adoption rates, and lifetime value
5. WHEN creating dashboards THEN the system SHALL provide Google Analytics dashboards, Google Data Studio reports, and automated weekly/monthly reporting

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a marketing operations manager, I want comprehensive tool recommendations and platform account setup strategies, so that I can efficiently manage all marketing activities with the right technology stack and properly configured accounts.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN selecting marketing tools THEN the system SHALL recommend specific tools for social media management, email marketing, analytics, content creation, and project management
2. WHEN setting up social media accounts THEN the system SHALL provide step-by-step account creation and optimization guides for each platform including verification strategies
3. WHEN configuring analytics THEN the system SHALL provide Google Analytics 4 setup instructions, conversion tracking implementation, and custom dashboard creation
4. WHEN integrating tools THEN the system SHALL ensure all marketing tools connect properly for unified reporting and workflow automation

### Requirement 9

**User Story:** As a content creator, I want sample content templates and examples, so that I can quickly create engaging posts, articles, and campaigns that align with our brand voice and marketing objectives.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN creating social media content THEN the system SHALL provide 50+ sample posts for each platform with captions, hashtags, and visual descriptions
2. WHEN writing blog articles THEN the system SHALL provide article templates, headline formulas, and complete sample articles for each content pillar
3. WHEN designing email campaigns THEN the system SHALL provide subject line templates, email copy examples, and sequence frameworks
4. WHEN developing ad copy THEN the system SHALL provide sample ad headlines, descriptions, and call-to-action variations for different platforms

### Requirement 10

**User Story:** As a bootstrap marketing director, I want a detailed zero-budget implementation timeline and resource allocation strategy, so that I can execute the marketing plan systematically using only free tools and organic growth tactics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN planning implementation THEN the system SHALL provide 6-month phased rollout with monthly milestones focused on organic growth strategies
2. WHEN allocating resources THEN the system SHALL prioritize time investment across organic social media (40%), content marketing (30%), community building (20%), and email marketing (10%) using only free tools
3. WHEN tracking progress THEN the system SHALL provide weekly and monthly checkpoints with organic growth metrics and engagement rates
4. WHEN scaling efforts THEN the system SHALL identify successful organic channels for increased time investment and community-driven growth opportunities
