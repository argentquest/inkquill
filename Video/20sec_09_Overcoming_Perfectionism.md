# 20-Second Video Script #9: Overcoming Perfectionism

## Video Title: "How I Stopped Being a Perfectionist Writer"

## Setup & Context:
- **Setting**: Writer's space showing evolution from perfectionist paralysis to productive flow
- **Props**: Deleted drafts, perfectionist journals, comparison with productive writing
- **Lighting**: Starts tense/stressed, transitions to calm and productive
- **Camera**: Emotional journey shots, personal and vulnerable
- **Background**: Personal writing space showing psychological journey

## Script Timeline:

**[0-5 seconds] - PERFECTIONIST PARALYSIS**
- **Visual**: Writer staring at blank page, deleting text repeatedly
- **Text Overlay**: "Perfectionism was killing my creativity"
- **Audio**: "I'd write one sentence, delete it, write another, delete it. Hours passed with nothing to show. Perfect or nothing, right?"

**[5-10 seconds] - THE COMPARISON TRAP**
- **Visual**: Comparing work to published authors, feeling inadequate
- **Text Overlay**: "Comparing my drafts to published books"
- **Audio**: "I'd compare my rough first drafts to polished, edited novels. Of course mine looked terrible! But I couldn't see past that."

**[10-15 seconds] - PERMISSION TO BE IMPERFECT**
- **Visual**: Starting to write imperfectly with AI encouragement, making progress
- **Text Overlay**: "AI helped me embrace 'good enough' drafts"
- **Audio**: "AI helped me see that first drafts are supposed to be messy. It gave me permission to write imperfectly and fix later."

**[15-20 seconds] - PRODUCTIVE FREEDOM**
- **Visual**: Confidently writing, completing projects, celebrating progress
- **Text Overlay**: "Now I finish stories instead of perfecting paragraphs ✨"
- **Audio**: "Now I complete stories instead of perfecting single paragraphs. Progress over perfection - it's life-changing!"
- **End Card**: "Embrace imperfection → inkandquill.io"
