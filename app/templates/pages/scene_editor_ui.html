AI Generation Modes Explained
Continue / Append

AI continues writing from where you left off, adding new content to the end of your existing text. Perfect for when you have a good start and want the AI to keep the story flowing.

Elaborate / Expand

Requires highlighted text. AI takes your selected text and expands it with more detail, depth, and richness. Great for turning brief descriptions into vivid, detailed scenes.

Rewrite

Requires highlighted text. AI rewrites your selected text in a different style, tone, or perspective while preserving the core meaning. Useful for improving clarity or changing voice.

Generate New

AI creates completely fresh content based on your instruction, ignoring existing text. Perfect for brainstorming new ideas, alternative scenes, or starting from scratch.

Tip: For "Elaborate/Expand" and "Rewrite" modes, highlight the text you want to work with before clicking Generate.
