Import Overview

The Document Import system allows you to bring research materials, notes, reference documents, and other supporting content into your storytelling workspace. These documents become part of your knowledge base, accessible to you and your AI assistant for enhanced writing support.

Key Features

Multiple Formats
Support for various document types and formats
Intelligent Processing
Automatic content extraction and organization
Search Integration
Full-text search across all imported documents
AI Context
Documents become available to AI for contextual assistance

Document Types

Import various types of documents to support your writing process:

Reference materials and research
  • Academic papers

  • Cultural guides

Writing Notes

Personal notes and ideas
  • Plot outlines
  • Character sketches
  • Scene ideas
  • Brainstorming notes

Text Documents:
  • .txt, .rtf, .md
  • .docx, .odt
Spreadsheets:

  • .csv files
Other Formats:
  • .pdf files
  • Web articles (URL)
