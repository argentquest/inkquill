Lore Item List Management

Organize and manage all lore items including artifacts, customs, laws, and historical events in your world.

Managing Lore Items:

  1. View all lore items organized by category
  2. Filter by type (artifacts, customs, laws, events)
  3. Sort by importance, creation date, or alphabetical order
  4. Use tags to group related lore items
  5. Create connections between lore items and other world elements

Lore Categories:

  • Artifacts: Magical items, weapons, and important objects
  • Customs: Traditions, ceremonies, and cultural practices
  • Laws: Rules, regulations, and governance systems
  • Events: Historical moments and turning points
  • Beliefs: Religions, philosophies, and ideologies
Interconnection: Link lore items to characters and locations. A famous sword might be wielded by a hero and kept in a castle.
World Consistency: Ensure lore items fit your world's established rules and timeline.
