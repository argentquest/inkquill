Editor Overview

The Basic Story Editor is designed for writers who want to focus purely on storytelling without the complexity of world-building. It provides a clean, distraction-free environment with powerful AI assistance to enhance your creativity.

🎯

Perfect For

  • Quick story ideas and short fiction
  • Personal narratives and memoirs
  • First drafts and creative experiments
  • Writing practice and exercises
  • Stories that don't require complex world-building

📊 Basic vs Advanced Stories

Feature Basic Editor Advanced Editor
Setup Time ✅ Instant ⏱️ Requires world creation
Structure ✅ Simple acts/scenes 📚 Full story hierarchy
Character Management 📝 Inline mentions 🎭 Detailed character profiles
AI Assistance ✅ Text enhancement 🧠 World-aware suggestions
Publishing ✅ One-click sharing 📖 Advanced publishing options

Formatting & Style

Keep your story readable and professionally formatted:

📝 Text Formatting

  • Bold: Ctrl+B or toolbar button
  • Italic: Ctrl+I or toolbar button
  • Underline: Ctrl+U or toolbar button
  • Headers: Use heading styles for chapters/sections

📋 Paragraph Styles

  • Normal Text: Standard story content
  • Dialogue: Character conversations
  • Internal Thought: Character thoughts (often italicized)
  • Scene Breaks: Use *** or ### to separate scenes

🎭 Dialogue Formatting

  • Use quotation marks for spoken words
  • Start new paragraph for each speaker
  • Include action beats between dialogue
  • Maintain consistent punctuation style

✨ Formatting Example

Chapter 1: The Beginning

The morning sun cast long shadows across the empty street.

"Are you sure about this?" Sarah asked, her voice barely above a whisper.

*This is it,* she thought to herself. *No turning back now.*

***

Hours later, she found herself standing at the crossroads...

Story Organization

While Basic Stories are simplified, you can still organize your content effectively:

📚 Simple Structure

  • Single Act: Your entire story in one document
  • Scenes: Use scene breaks (***) to separate major sections
  • Chapters: Use heading styles to mark chapter divisions
  • Parts: For longer works, use larger headings for major parts

🗂️ Organization Tips

  • Start each chapter with a clear heading
  • Use consistent scene break markers
  • Keep related scenes together
  • Consider time and location when organizing
  • Use the find feature to navigate quickly

Tips & Best Practices

🚀 Writing Efficiently

  • Start Writing First: Don't perfect the first sentence—get your ideas flowing
  • Use AI for Enhancement: Write your draft, then use AI to improve specific sections
  • Focus on Story: Don't get distracted by formatting until your first draft is complete
  • Set Goals: Use the word count feature to track daily writing targets

📚 Story Development

  • Start Simple: Begin with a clear protagonist and conflict
  • Show Don't Tell: Use action and dialogue to reveal character and plot
  • Maintain Pace: Balance action, dialogue, and description
  • End Strong: Ensure your conclusion feels satisfying and complete

🤖 AI Collaboration

  • Be Specific: Give AI clear context about what you want to improve
  • Maintain Voice: Edit AI suggestions to match your writing style
  • Use for Inspiration: AI suggestions can spark new ideas and directions
  • Review Everything: Always read and approve AI-generated content

💡 Overcoming Challenges

  • Writer's Block: Use AI prompts or write about what happens next
  • Dialogue Issues: Read dialogue aloud to test naturalness
  • Pacing Problems: Use scene breaks to control story rhythm
  • Ending Difficulties: Work backward from your desired conclusion
🏆

Success Strategy

The Basic Story Editor is designed for momentum. Focus on getting your story down first, then use the available tools to polish and perfect your work. Remember: every great story starts with a single sentence!
