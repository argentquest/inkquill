📖 Overview

The Published Stories Gallery is a community space where writers share their completed stories with other users. Here you can:

  • Discover new stories created by other community members
  • Browse by genre, popularity, or publication date
  • Read and comment on stories that interest you
  • Find inspiration for your own writing projects

🔍 Browsing Stories

Each story card displays:

  • Cover Image - A 256x256 square image representing the story
  • Title and Author - The story name and who created it
  • Story Type - Basic (quick narrative) or Advanced (full world-building)
  • Description - A brief summary of the story
  • Stats - View count, comments, and ratings
  • Publication Date - When the story was shared
💡 Tip: Click anywhere on a story card to view it, or use the "View" button for the same action.

🔍 Search & Filters

Use the search and filter tools to find specific stories:

Search Options

  • Text Search - Search by title, description, or author name
  • Sort Options:
    • Recently Created - Newest stories first
    • Most Popular - Based on view count
    • Highest Rated - Top-rated stories
    • Alphabetical - A-Z by title
🔍 Search Tips:
  • Use keywords from the story title or description
  • Try author usernames to find stories by specific writers
  • Combine search with sorting for better results

👁️ Viewing Stories

When you click on a story card or the "View" button, you'll be taken to the story's detail page where you can:

  • Read the full story with proper formatting
  • See story metadata like word count and world information
  • Leave comments and engage with other readers
  • Rate the story to help others discover quality content
  • Share the story with others
📝 Note: Comments and ratings help build a supportive community and provide valuable feedback to authors.

✍️ Publishing Your Own Stories

Ready to share your own stories with the community?

How to Publish

  1. Create a story using the "Your Stories" section
  2. Complete your story with all acts and scenes
  3. Review and edit your content
  4. Use the publish option in your story's settings
  5. Add a description and cover image if desired
  6. Submit for publication to the gallery
📚 Publishing Tips:
  • Write a compelling description to attract readers
  • Add a cover image to make your story stand out
  • Check your story for typos and formatting
  • Choose an appropriate title that reflects your story's content

🤝 Community Guidelines

Help maintain a positive and supportive environment:

  • Be respectful in comments and interactions
  • Provide constructive feedback to help authors improve
  • Report inappropriate content if you encounter it
  • Support fellow writers by reading and commenting on their work
  • Share quality content that others will enjoy
