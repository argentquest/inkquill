🏠 Dashboard Overview

Welcome to your AI Storytelling Assistant dashboard! This is your central hub for creating worlds, writing stories, and managing your creative projects with the power of AI.

ℹ️
New to AI Storytelling?
Start by creating a world first - it provides the context that makes your AI assistant truly intelligent and helpful.

Getting Started: Your 3-Step Journey

🌍 Build Your World
Create your story universe using the World Builder Wizard (recommended for beginners), import from existing literature, or start from scratch. Your world provides context that makes the AI understand your creative vision.
✨ Start World Wizard 🌍 My Worlds
📚 Create Your Story
Link your story to a world, then structure it with acts and scenes. The AI writing assistant provides real-time help based on your world's context, characters, and lore.
📖 My Stories ➕ New Story
🤖 Explore AI Features
Generate characters with the AI Character Generator, create rich locations and lore items, upload documents for context, and chat with AI about your world for inspiration.

💡 Pro Tip: Quick Start
New users should try the "Import from Book" feature first! Enter a title like "The Lord of the Rings" or "Harry Potter" to see how the AI creates a complete world with characters, locations, and lore items. It's the fastest way to understand the system's power.
🚀 5-Minute Quick Start
⏱️ 5 minutes
  1. 1
    Create a World: Click "Import from Book" and enter "Alice in Wonderland"
  2. 2
    Wait for Processing: The AI will extract characters, locations, and lore (2-3 minutes)
  3. 3
    Create a Story: Link it to your new world and start writing with AI assistance
  4. 4
    Try AI Writing: Use the Act Editor's "AI Assistant" tab to collaborate with your AI co-author
