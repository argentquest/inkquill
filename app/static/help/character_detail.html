AI Character Development
AI can help expand basic character concepts into fully realized personalities with detailed backstories, motivations, and unique traits.

Enhancement Options:

  • Personality Expansion: Generate detailed personality profiles from basic traits
  • Backstory Generation: Create rich histories that explain character motivations
  • Dialogue Voice: Develop unique speaking patterns and vocabulary
  • Physical Details: Add specific appearance features and mannerisms
  • Relationship Dynamics: Generate complex interpersonal connections

Tips & Best Practices

💡 Character Development
Start with core traits and let AI help flesh out the details. The more specific your initial input, the better the AI suggestions will be.
🎭 Authentic Characters
Give characters flaws and contradictions. Perfect characters are boring - internal conflicts make them relatable and interesting.
🔗 Character Relationships
Define how characters relate to each other before they interact in your story. This creates natural tension and dialogue.
