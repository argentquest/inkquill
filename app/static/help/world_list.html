Pro Tips

What is the World List?

The World List is your central dashboard for managing all your creative universes. Each world contains characters, locations, lore items, and stories - everything you need for rich, context-aware AI storytelling.

🎯
Why Worlds Matter
Worlds are the foundation of intelligent AI assistance. The more detailed your world, the better the AI understands your creative vision and provides relevant suggestions.

🚀 Quick Start: World Builder Wizard
⏱️ 5 minutes
  1. 1
    Start the Wizard: Click "World Builder Wizard" on your worlds page
  2. 2
    Answer Questions: Describe your world's genre, technology level, magic system, and key themes
  3. 3
    Review Preview: Check the AI-generated world description and make adjustments
  4. 4
    Name Your World: Give it a memorable name and create your universe
  5. 5
    Explore & Expand: Start adding characters, locations, and stories to bring it to life
💡 First-Time Builder Tip
Start with a simple concept for your first world. You can always expand and add complexity later as you become more familiar with the tools. Think of it as building a foundation that you'll develop over time.

Managing Your Worlds

Once you have worlds, you can organize and manage them effectively:

Understanding World Cards

Each world appears as a card displaying key information:

  • 🌍 Title & Description: Your world's name and summary
  • 📊 Element Counts: Characters, locations, lore items, and stories you've created
  • 🔒 Privacy Status: Private (only you), shared, or public
  • ⏰ Last Updated: When you last made changes
  • 🎨 World Image: Visual representation (if you've added one)

World Management Features

Quick Actions

Edit, duplicate, or delete worlds directly from the list. Hover over a world card to see action buttons.

Privacy Controls

Toggle between private (only you), shared (specific users), or public (everyone can view).

Search & Filter

Use the search bar to find worlds by name or description. Filter by privacy level or creation date.

World Builder Wizard

The green "Generate New World" button opens an interactive wizard that guides you through 20 questions to create a detailed world automatically.

Element Count Indicators

The colored badges on each world card show:

Badge Element Type What It Contains
👥 Characters People & Beings Protagonists, antagonists, NPCs, and creatures
🏰 Locations Places Cities, buildings, rooms, and regions
📜 Lore Items World Knowledge Magic systems, artifacts, history, and customs
📚 Stories Narratives Complete stories set in this world

Advanced Power User Features

Bulk Operations ▼

World Duplication Strategies ▼

Use world duplication for:

  • Alternate Timelines: Create "what if" scenarios
  • Version Control: Keep backups before major changes
  • Template Creation: Build reusable world foundations
⚠️
Cost Note: Duplicating worlds with many AI-generated elements will consume credits for regenerating embeddings and search indices.
API Integration ▼

Advanced users can integrate with external tools:

GET /api/worlds
POST /api/worlds
PUT /api/worlds/{id}
DELETE /api/worlds/{id}

Performance Optimization

For large world collections:

  • Pagination: Only 20 worlds load at once by default
  • Lazy Loading: World details load when needed
  • Search Indexing: Real-time search across all content
