Beginner What is World Detail?

Worlds are the foundation of your storytelling ecosystem. They contain all your characters, locations, lore items, and provide context for AI-assisted writing. The World Detail page is your central hub for managing and developing your universe.

Intermediate Managing World Elements

Characters ▼

Create and manage the people who populate your world:

  • Protagonists: Main characters who drive your story
  • Antagonists: Characters who create conflict and obstacles
  • Supporting Characters: Secondary characters who enrich the narrative
  • NPCs: Background characters who populate your world

Creating Rich Characters

15 minutes
  1. 1
    Start with basic information: name, age, occupation
  2. 2
    Define personality traits and core motivations
  3. 3
    Add physical description and appearance details
  4. 4
    Develop backstory and personal history
  5. 5
    Associate with relevant locations and other characters
Locations ▼

Build the places where your story unfolds:

  • Cities and Towns: Urban centers with their own culture and politics
  • Natural Locations: Forests, mountains, rivers, and wilderness areas
  • Buildings: Specific structures like taverns, castles, or spaceships
  • Regions: Larger geographical or political areas
Location Type Key Details AI Benefits
Cities Population, culture, government, districts Rich scene setting, cultural context
Buildings Layout, atmosphere, purpose, inhabitants Detailed scene descriptions
Wilderness Geography, climate, dangers, resources Environmental storytelling
Regions Borders, politics, trade, conflicts Political context, travel logistics
Lore Items ▼

Document the deeper elements of your world:

  • Organizations: Guilds, governments, secret societies
  • Artifacts: Magical items, ancient relics, technology
  • Customs: Cultural practices, traditions, ceremonies
  • History: Past events, wars, discoveries
  • Languages: Communication systems and linguistic details
  • Magic Systems: How supernatural powers work in your world

Advanced AI-Enhanced World Features

World Chat ▼

Discuss your world with AI to develop ideas and resolve issues:

  • Plot Development: Work through story problems with AI guidance
  • World Consistency: Check for contradictions in your world-building
  • Character Development: Explore character motivations and relationships
  • Research Assistance: Get suggestions for historical or scientific accuracy
💰 Cost: 5-15 credits per conversation, depending on complexity and length
Character Generator ▼

Create detailed characters using AI assistance:

  • Personality Generation: AI creates believable personality traits
  • Backstory Development: Generate rich character histories
  • Physical Descriptions: Detailed appearance and mannerisms
  • Voice and Dialogue: Examples of how the character speaks
Image Generation ▼

Create visual representations of your world elements:

  • Character Portraits: Visualize how your characters look
  • Location Art: Generate landscape and architectural images
  • Item Illustrations: Create images of artifacts and objects
  • World Maps: Generate geographical representations
💰 Cost: 15-30 credits per image, depending on complexity and style
Document Integration ▼

Upload research materials to enhance AI context:

  • Reference Materials: Historical documents, cultural research
  • Character Sheets: Detailed character templates and profiles
  • World Bibles: Comprehensive world-building documents
  • Map Files: Geographical and political maps

World Management Tools

Hierarchy View ▼

Visualize relationships between world elements:

  • Character Relationships: Family trees, friendships, rivalries
  • Location Hierarchies: Cities within regions, buildings within cities
  • Organization Structures: Guild hierarchies, government levels
  • Timeline Connections: Historical events and their impacts
World Map ▼

Interactive map showing location connections:

  • Geographical Layout: Visual representation of your world
  • Location Markers: Pin important places and landmarks
  • Travel Routes: Show paths between locations
  • Political Boundaries: Mark territories and regions
Export and Backup ▼

Protect and share your world data:

  • World Export: Download complete world data as JSON or PDF
  • Element Sharing: Share specific characters or locations
  • Backup Creation: Regular automatic backups of your world
  • Version Control: Track changes and restore previous versions

Advanced World Building Best Practices

Effective World Development ▼

Strategies for creating rich, consistent worlds:

  • Start Small: Begin with essential elements and expand gradually
  • Focus on Story Needs: Develop elements that serve your narrative
  • Maintain Consistency: Regular consistency checks prevent contradictions
  • Layer Details: Add depth progressively rather than all at once
  • Document Everything: Keep detailed records of all decisions
AI Optimization Tips ▼

Get the most from AI features:

  • Detailed Descriptions: More detail provides better AI context
  • Clear Relationships: Define how elements connect to each other
  • Regular Updates: Keep world elements current with story progress
  • Strategic Associations: Link elements that should influence each other
  • Quality over Quantity: Better to have fewer, well-developed elements
Collaboration and Sharing ▼

Work with others on world development:

  • World Sharing: Grant access to co-authors and beta readers
  • Element Permissions: Control what others can view or edit
  • Comment System: Collect feedback on world elements
  • Version Tracking: See who made what changes when
  • Export Tools: Share formatted documents with stakeholders

30 minutes
  1. 1

  2. 2
    Add your protagonist - start with the most important character
  3. 3
    Create the primary setting where your story begins
  4. 4
    Add any important organizations or magic systems
  5. 5
    Test AI writing assistance to see how context helps
  6. 6
    Expand elements as your story develops
Pro World-Building Tip
Your world should feel lived-in and authentic. Focus on how ordinary people experience your world's unique elements. The small details often make the biggest impact on reader immersion.
