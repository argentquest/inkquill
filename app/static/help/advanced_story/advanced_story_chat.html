What is Advanced Story Chat?

Advanced Story Chat provides comprehensive AI assistance for complex storytelling projects. The AI has access to your entire world-building database including characters, locations, lore items, and uploaded documents, enabling sophisticated discussions about narrative consistency, character relationships, and world development.

How to Use Advanced Story Chat
  1. Access Chat: Go to your story detail page and click "Show Chat"
  2. Create Session: Click "New Chat" and choose a focus area:
    • Plot - Story structure, conflicts, resolutions
    • Characters - Character development, relationships, arcs
    • World - World-building, consistency, expansion
    • Themes - Thematic elements, messages, symbolism
    • Structure - Act/scene organization, pacing
    • Writing - Technical writing craft and style
    • General - Open-ended comprehensive discussion
  3. Target Content: Select specific acts, scenes, or story elements
  4. Ask Complex Questions: Leverage the AI's full world knowledge
  5. Get Comprehensive Feedback: Receive world-aware, context-rich responses

Troubleshooting

  • Slow loading: Large worlds may take time to load context
  • Timeouts: Complex queries may need more processing time
  • Memory issues: Very large worlds may hit context limits
  • Connection drops: Check internet stability for WebSocket connection

  • Missing context: Ensure characters/locations are properly defined
  • Inconsistent responses: Check for conflicting information in world data
  • Generic answers: Try more specific questions about world elements
  • Outdated information: Update world elements if story has changed
