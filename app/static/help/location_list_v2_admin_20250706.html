Filtering & Sorting

Use powerful filtering and sorting options to quickly find the locations you need.

Filter Options:

  • Location Type: Cities, buildings, natural areas, dungeons, etc.
  • Size: Small, medium, large, or massive locations
  • Importance: Major, minor, or background locations
  • Tags: Custom tags you've applied to locations
  • Creation Date: Recently added or older locations
  • Has Image: Locations with or without generated images

Sort Options:

  • Alphabetical: A-Z or Z-A by name
  • Creation Date: Newest first or oldest first
  • Last Modified: Recently updated locations
  • Importance: Most important locations first
  • Size: Largest to smallest or vice versa

Best Practices

🏠 Location Hierarchy
Establish clear relationships between locations. A tavern should belong to a city, which belongs to a region. This creates a logical world structure.
⚠️ Consistency Check
Keep location sizes and distances realistic relative to each other. A village shouldn't be larger than a city in the same region.
📝 Naming Conventions
Use consistent naming patterns for similar location types. This helps readers navigate your world and creates a cohesive feel.
