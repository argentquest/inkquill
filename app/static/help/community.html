📚 Sharing Your Stories

Share your creative work with the community and discover stories from other writers:

Publishing Your Story

  • Use the "Publish" button in your story editor
  • Choose your visibility settings (public, community only, etc.)
  • Add a compelling description to attract readers
  • Tag your story with relevant categories

Discovering Stories

  • Browse the published stories section
  • Use filters to find stories by genre, length, or rating
  • Follow your favorite authors for updates
  • Create reading lists for stories you want to read later

🎨 Getting and Giving Feedback

Constructive feedback is essential for improving your writing. Here's how our feedback system works:

Requesting Feedback

  • Post your story or excerpt in the feedback section
  • Be specific about what kind of feedback you're looking for
  • Mention if you want feedback on plot, characters, pacing, etc.
  • Be open to constructive criticism

Giving Feedback

  • Always be constructive and respectful
  • Point out both strengths and areas for improvement
  • Be specific in your comments
  • Offer suggestions for improvement

📋 Community Guidelines

To maintain a positive and supportive environment, please follow these guidelines:

🤝 Be Respectful

Treat all community members with respect and kindness

🎯 Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to writing and storytelling

📝 Original Content

Only share content that you own or have permission to use

🚫 No Spam

Avoid excessive self-promotion or repetitive posts

🔒 Privacy

Respect others' privacy and don't share personal information

⚖️ Fair Use

Follow copyright laws and give credit where due
