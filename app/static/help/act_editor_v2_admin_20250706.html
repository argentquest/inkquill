Understanding the Editor Interface

Split-Screen Layout
The editor features a Writing Area (left side) for your manuscript and an AI Assistant Panel (right side) for AI interactions and suggestions.
AI Assistant Tabs
Switch between different AI functions: Generate (content creation), Review (feedback), and Chat (brainstorming).
Toolbar Functions
Access formatting, save options, word count, and quick AI actions from the toolbar above your writing area.
Preview and Incorporate
AI suggestions appear in a preview box. Review, edit if needed, then click "Incorporate" to add content to your manuscript.

AI Generation Modes

Choose the right AI mode for your specific writing needs:

➡️ Continue/Append
Most Popular

💭 Custom Instruction
Specific Control
Use specific instructions for targeted changes. Example: "Add dialogue showing tension" or "Describe the magic system in action."
  • Precise control over output
  • Targeted scene development
  • Specific character interactions
🎯 Effective AI Prompting
⏱️ Improve results instantly
  1. 1
    Be Specific: Instead of "write more," try "write dialogue between John and Sarah where John reveals his secret"
  2. 2
    Include Context: Mention emotions, location, or recent events: "Sarah is angry after discovering the truth"
  3. 3
    Reference Characters: Use character names for voice consistency: "Write this in Marcus's confident speaking style"
  4. 4
    Set the Tone: Specify mood: "Make this tense and suspenseful" or "Add humor to lighten the scene"

Writing Best Practices

Collaboration Workflow

Start with Your Voice
Write the opening of each scene or chapter yourself to establish tone, then use AI to help develop and expand.
Use AI for Momentum
When you're stuck, ask AI to continue or suggest what might happen next. Use its ideas as springboards for your own creativity.
Edit AI Suggestions
Always review and personalize AI output. Adjust word choices, add your unique voice, and ensure it matches your vision.
Iterate and Refine
Use multiple AI generations with different instructions to explore options, then combine the best elements.
💡
The 80/20 Rule
Aim for 80% your own writing and 20% AI assistance. This maintains your unique voice while leveraging AI for inspiration, momentum, and enhancement.

Tips & Troubleshooting

⚠️
Common Issues
If AI suggestions seem off-target, check that your story is properly linked to a world and that character details are complete.

Improving AI Performance

  • 🎯 Be Specific: Detailed prompts yield better results than vague requests
  • 📚 Rich World Data: More character and world details improve AI context
  • 🔄 Iterate: Try multiple variations to find the best approach
  • ✂️ Edit Liberally: Use AI output as raw material for your own refinement
  • 💾 Save Frequently: Use Ctrl+S or rely on auto-save for peace of mind
