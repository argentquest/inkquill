🎭 Character Creation Best Practices
⏱️ Time: 10-30 minutes per character
  1. 1
    Define Role First: Establish the character's function in your story (protagonist, antagonist, mentor, comic relief)
  2. 2
    Core Personality: Choose 3-5 key personality traits that drive their decisions and conflicts
  3. 3
    Motivation & Goals: What does this character want most? What drives their actions?
  4. 4
    Voice & Dialogue: Add examples of how they speak to help AI maintain consistent character voice
  5. 5
    Relationships: Connect them to other characters, locations, and world elements for rich context

Organization & Best Practices

Character Organization Strategies

📁 By Story Role
Group characters by their narrative function:
  • Protagonists (main characters)
  • Antagonists (opposition forces)
  • Supporting (friends, allies, mentors)
  • NPCs (background population)
⭐ By Importance
Organize by narrative significance:
  • Major (central to main plot)
  • Minor (important subplots)
  • Background (world flavor)
🏰 By Location
Group by geographic or social location:
  • Royal court characters
  • Village inhabitants
  • Traveling merchants
  • Wilderness dwellers
📖 By Story Arc
Organize by when they appear:
  • Act 1 introductions
  • Mid-story arrivals
  • Climax participants
  • Epilogue appearances
💡 Organization Pro Tip
Use consistent naming conventions and maintain a main character roster. For fantasy: "Sir Marcus the Bold" vs modern: "Marcus Rodriguez, Detective". Consistency helps both you and the AI navigate your character roster efficiently.

Character Development Workflow

  1. Start Simple: Begin with name, role, and one key trait
  2. Add Depth Gradually: Expand personality, backstory, and relationships over time
  3. Test in Stories: Use characters in writing to see how they feel
  4. Refine Based on Use: Adjust personality and voice based on story needs
  5. Connect to World: Link to locations, other characters, and lore items
  6. Update Regularly: Keep character details current as stories evolve
