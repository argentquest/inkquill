Create World from Document

Transform research documents, worldbuilding notes, and creative materials into organized world elements.

Document Import Process:

  1. Upload your document (PDF, DOCX, TXT, or MD)
  2. Choose content type (worldbuilding notes, research, outline)
  3. AI categorizes and structures the content
  4. Review suggested characters, locations, and lore
  5. Edit and organize the generated world elements

Document Types:

  • Worldbuilding Notes: Existing character and location descriptions
  • Research Materials: Historical or cultural reference documents
  • Story Outlines: Plot summaries and character arcs
  • Campaign Guides: RPG materials and game settings
  • Creative Fragments: Scattered ideas and inspiration
Optimization: Well-organized documents with clear headings and structure produce better results.
Review Required: Always review AI-generated content for accuracy and consistency with your vision.
