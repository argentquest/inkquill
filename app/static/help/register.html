Password Security Guidelines ▼

Create a strong password to protect your creative work:

  • Length: At least 8 characters (12+ recommended)
  • Complexity: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Uniqueness: Don't reuse passwords from other sites
  • Avoid: Personal information, common words, or predictable patterns
  • Consider: Using a password manager for unique, secure passwords
Strong Password Examples:
✅ Good: MyStory2024!Write
✅ Good: Dragon$Quest#47
✅ Good: Creative&Mind88

❌ Avoid: password123
❌ Avoid: yourname2024
❌ Avoid: 12345678

Beginner Email Verification Process

Verification Steps ▼

After registration, you'll need to verify your email address:

  1. Check Your Email: Look for a message from the AI Storytelling Assistant
  2. Click Verification Link: Click the link in the email to confirm your address
  3. Return to Platform: You'll be redirected back to complete setup
  4. Access Granted: Your account is now fully activated
📧
Check Spam Folder: Verification emails sometimes end up in spam. Check all folders if you don't see the email within 5 minutes.
Verification Issues ▼

Common verification problems and solutions:

Problem Cause Solution
No verification email Email in spam or wrong address Check spam folder, request new email
Link expired Waited too long to verify Request new verification email
Link doesn't work Email client altered link Copy/paste full URL into browser
Wrong email used Typo during registration Contact support to update email

Intermediate Registration Troubleshooting

Common Registration Issues ▼
Issue Likely Cause Solution
"Email already exists" Account already created Try logging in or use password reset
"Invalid email format" Email address format error Check for typos, ensure proper format
"Password too weak" Password doesn't meet requirements Use 8+ characters with numbers/symbols
"Display name taken" Username already in use Try variations or add numbers

Browser and Technical Requirements ▼

Ensure your browser supports all platform features:

  • Supported Browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
  • JavaScript: Must be enabled for full functionality
  • Cookies: Required for login and session management
  • Internet Connection: Stable connection recommended for AI features
  • Screen Resolution: 1024x768 minimum, responsive on mobile
Account Recovery Options ▼

If you're having trouble accessing your account:

  • Password Reset: Use "Forgot Password" link on login page
  • Email Issues: Contact support to update email address
  • Account Lockout: Wait 15 minutes or contact support
  • Technical Problems: Try incognito/private browsing mode
  • Account Deletion: Contact support to restore deleted accounts
Contact Support ▼

When to contact support and what information to include:

  • Email Issues: Include attempted email address
  • Technical Errors: Include browser type and error messages
  • Account Problems: Include display name and registration date
  • Feature Issues: Include steps to reproduce the problem
📧
Support Response: We typically respond to registration issues within 24 hours. Check your spam folder for our reply.
