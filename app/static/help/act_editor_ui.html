Beginner Editor Interface Overview

The Act Editor provides a focused writing environment where you can craft major story sections with AI assistance. It combines traditional text editing with intelligent suggestions based on your world context.

📝 Main Editor

Rich text editing area with formatting tools, word count, and real-time AI suggestions.

🎯 Context Panel

Shows associated characters, locations, and lore relevant to this act.

🤖 AI Assistant

Real-time writing help, suggestions, and content generation tools.

📊 Progress Tracker

Word count goals, completion percentage, and writing statistics.

First Time Using the Editor

5 minutes
  1. 1
    Review act metadata (title, summary, goals) in the header
  2. 2
    Check the context panel for associated world elements
  3. 3
    Set word count goals using the progress tracker
  4. 4
    Start typing in the main editor - AI will offer suggestions
  5. 5
    Use Ctrl+S regularly to save your progress

Intermediate AI Writing Assistance

🧠 Contextual Writing AI

The AI understands your world context, character relationships, and story direction to provide relevant, consistent writing assistance.

Real-Time Suggestions ▼

As you write, AI provides:

  • Auto-Complete: Finishes sentences based on context
  • Paragraph Extensions: Continues narrative flow
  • Character Dialogue: Voice-appropriate speech for each character
  • Scene Descriptions: Rich environmental and emotional details
  • Transition Phrases: Smooth connections between ideas

Using AI Suggestions Effectively

3 minutes
  1. 1
    Write 1-2 sentences to establish context
  2. 2
    Pause at natural break points (end of sentence/paragraph)
  3. 3
    Wait for AI suggestion to appear (gray text)
  4. 4
    Press Tab to accept, or keep typing to dismiss
  5. 5
    Edit accepted suggestions to match your voice
💰 Cost: 1-2 credits per suggestion, 3-5 credits per paragraph generation
Content Generation Tools ▼

Access advanced AI tools via the toolbar:

  • Expand Scene: Generates detailed content for outlined events
  • Character Development: Adds depth to character moments
  • Dialogue Enhancement: Improves conversation flow and subtext
  • Action Sequences: Creates dynamic, engaging action scenes
  • Emotional Beats: Develops character emotional arcs
AI Generation Prompts:
"Expand this outline into a full scene"
"Add more tension to this dialogue"
"Describe the setting in more detail"
"Show character emotions through actions"
"Create a smooth transition to the next scene"
Context Awareness ▼

AI leverages your world knowledge:

  • Character Consistency: Maintains established personalities and relationships
  • Location Details: Uses associated location descriptions and atmosphere
  • Lore Integration: Incorporates relevant world knowledge naturally
  • Story Continuity: References previous events and character development
  • Tone Matching: Maintains the established story tone and genre
✨
Pro Tip: The more detailed your character and location associations, the better AI suggestions become. Invest time in world-building for richer AI assistance.

Advanced Writing Best Practices

Effective AI Collaboration ▼

Get the best results from AI assistance:

  • Clear Direction: Start with strong opening sentences to guide AI
  • Specific Context: Include character emotions and motivations
  • Review and Edit: Always review AI suggestions for consistency
  • Personal Voice: Edit AI content to match your writing style
  • Incremental Building: Work in small chunks rather than large generations
Good AI Prompts:
"Sarah felt betrayed as she opened the letter..."
"The ancient library held secrets that..."
"During the tense negotiation, Marcus..."

Avoid vague starts:
"Something happened..."
"There was a place..."
"A person did..."
Act Structure Guidelines ▼

Structure your acts for maximum impact:

  • Clear Purpose: Each act should advance the story meaningfully
  • Character Focus: Center acts around character development or conflict
  • Pacing Variety: Mix action, dialogue, and reflection
  • Scene Breaks: Use natural transitions between different scenes
  • Cliffhangers: End acts with hooks to maintain reader engagement
⚠️
Common Pitfall: Don't try to include everything in one act. Focus on a specific story thread or character arc per act.
Maintaining Consistency ▼

Keep your narrative coherent:

  • Character Voice: Each character should speak and think distinctly
  • World Rules: Follow established magic systems, technology, and physics
  • Timeline: Track time progression and character ages
  • Relationship Dynamics: Maintain established character relationships
  • Emotional Arcs: Ensure character growth feels natural and earned
Productivity Tips ▼

Write more effectively:

  • Set Daily Goals: Use word count targets to maintain momentum
  • Write First, Edit Later: Get the story down before perfecting prose
  • Use Placeholders: Mark research needs with [RESEARCH: topic] tags
  • Regular Breaks: Step away periodically to maintain fresh perspective
  • Read Aloud: Catch awkward phrasing and rhythm issues
💰 Cost Management: Use AI for first drafts and difficult scenes, then edit manually to control credit usage.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Problem Solution
AI suggestions don't fit my style Edit accepted suggestions; AI learns from your edits
Characters acting out of character Check character associations and add more personality details
Repetitive AI suggestions Add more context or try different phrasing for variety
Slow AI response Check internet connection; try shorter request blocks
Lost work after refresh Check auto-save; restore from version history if needed
