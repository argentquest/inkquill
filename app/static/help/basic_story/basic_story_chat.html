Basic Story Chat - AI Writing Assistance
Basic Story Focus: Chat discussions focus on writing craft and storytelling techniques without complex world-building elements.
What is Basic Story Chat?

Basic Story Chat provides real-time AI assistance focused on helping you improve your writing and develop your story's narrative. The AI acts as a writing coach, offering suggestions on plot development, character arcs, pacing, and other storytelling elements.

Key Features Available
Available Features
  • Writing craft guidance
  • Plot development assistance
  • Character development (narrative focus)
  • Pacing and flow suggestions
  • Dialogue improvement
  • Creative brainstorming
  • Story structure guidance
  • Full act/scene content access
Not Available (Advanced Only)
  • Character sheet discussions
  • Location world-building
  • Lore item development
  • World consistency checks
  • World hierarchy discussions
  • RAG context integration
How to Use Story Chat
  1. Access Chat: Go to your story detail page and click "Show Chat"
  2. Create Session: Click "New Chat" and choose a focus area:
    • Plot - Story structure, conflicts, resolutions
    • Writing - Techniques, style, voice
    • Structure - Act/scene organization
    • General - Open-ended discussion
  3. Target Content: Select specific acts or scenes to discuss
  4. Ask Questions: Type your questions or requests for assistance
  5. Get Feedback: Receive AI responses focused on writing improvement
Example Chat Topics
Plot Development
  • "How can I make this conflict more engaging?"
  • "What's missing from this scene?"
  • "How should I resolve this plot thread?"
Writing Craft
  • "How can I improve this dialogue?"
  • "Is the pacing too slow here?"
  • "How do I show instead of tell?"
Character Development
  • "How can I make this character more compelling?"
  • "What motivates this character?"
  • "How do I show character growth?"
AI Context & Capabilities
What the AI Knows About Your Story
  • Complete Story Content: Full text of all acts and scenes
  • Story Structure: How your acts and scenes are organized
  • Writing Focus: Emphasis on storytelling techniques
  • Context-Free Approach: No world-building complexity
Want More Features?
Upgrade to Advanced Story to unlock:
  • Character sheet discussions and development
  • Location world-building and consistency
  • Lore item integration and mythology
  • World hierarchy and relationship mapping
  • RAG context from uploaded documents
You can upgrade any Basic Story to Advanced from the story detail page.
Cost & Usage
  • Token Usage: All chat interactions count toward your token usage
  • Cost Tracking: Each AI response is logged with estimated cost
  • Context Efficiency: Basic stories use fewer tokens (no world context)
  • Billing: View usage in your account billing section
Troubleshooting

  • Chat not loading: Refresh the page and try again
  • Connection dropped: Check your internet connection
  • Messages not sending: Verify the chat session is active

  • Slow responses: AI is processing your story content
  • Generic responses: Try asking more specific questions
  • Off-topic responses: Ensure your question relates to writing/storytelling
