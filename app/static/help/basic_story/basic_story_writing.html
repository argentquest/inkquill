Simple Story Structure

Automatic Setup: Your first act is created automatically when you create a Basic Story. Act numbers are hidden when you only have one act - they appear when you add more.

Acts (Chapters)

  • Major story sections
  • Can contain multiple scenes
  • Support AI summaries
  • Unlimited acts allowed

Scenes

  • Smaller story segments
  • Live within acts
  • Individual AI assistance
  • Easy to reorganize

Writing Tips & Best Practices

Quick Start Strategy

Start writing immediately. Don't plan too much - Basic Stories are designed for creative flow. Use AI assistance when you get stuck or need inspiration.

Use AI Wisely

AI suggestions are context-free in Basic Stories. Use them for inspiration and enhancement, but maintain your unique voice and style throughout.

Writing Workflow

1
Write First Draft: Focus on getting your ideas down without editing
2
Use AI for Ideas: Ask for suggestions when you need direction
3
Enhance with AI: Improve dialogue, descriptions, and flow
4
Generate Summaries: Create AI summaries for acts and scenes
5
Final Review: Polish and prepare for publishing
