Basic Stories Overview

Streamlined writing for instant creativity
What are Basic Stories?
Basic Stories are a streamlined way to start writing immediately without the complexity of world-building. Perfect for quick story ideas, simple narratives, or when you just want to focus on writing.

What You Get

Simple Creation: Just title and description needed
Automatic Setup: First chapter created automatically
AI Writing Help: Context-free assistance and suggestions
Publishing: Full publishing capabilities (identical to Advanced Stories)
Unlimited Writing: No word limits or restrictions

What's Different from Advanced Stories

Basic Stories focus purely on writing. The following features are available only in Advanced Stories:
Character management and profiles
Location tracking and details
Story hierarchy view
World-building tools
Advanced AI with world context

⬆️ Upgrading to Advanced

Ready for more features?
You can upgrade any Basic Story to an Advanced Story at any time. This reveals world-building tools and unlocks all advanced features. The upgrade process is simple and cannot be undone.

When to Upgrade:

  • You want to add characters and locations
  • Your story is getting complex and needs organization
  • You want to use advanced world-building tools
  • You need AI assistance with world context

🔧 Getting Started

  1. Click "Start Writing" from the main dashboard
  2. Enter your story title and optional description
  3. Choose a template (optional) or start blank
  4. Click "Start Writing" to begin immediately
  5. Write your story using the simple editor
💡 Pro Tip: Don't overthink it! Basic Stories are designed for quick starts and creative flow. You can always add complexity later by upgrading to an Advanced Story.
