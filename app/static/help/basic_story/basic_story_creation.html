📝 Creating Basic Stories

Quick Overview:
Creating a Basic Story takes just 30 seconds. You'll need only a title and optional description to get started writing immediately.

🚀 Step-by-Step Creation

Step 1: Choose Creation Method

You can create a Basic Story from several places:

  • Dashboard: Click "Start Writing" or "Create Basic Story"
  • Stories Page: Click "New Basic Story" button
  • Navigation Menu: Use "Quick Actions" → "Start Writing"

Step 2: Enter Story Details

Required:

  • Title: Your story's name (can be changed later)

Optional:

  • Description: Brief summary of your story idea

Step 3: Auto-Creation Process

The system automatically creates:

  • ✅ A hidden "shadow world" (you won't see this)
  • ✅ Your Basic Story with the title and description
  • ✅ First act titled "Act 1" ready for writing
  • ✅ Basic writing interface without complexity

Step 4: Start Writing!

You'll be taken directly to the writing interface where you can:

  • Write your story content
  • Use AI assistance for ideas and suggestions
  • Add scenes within your act
  • Generate AI summaries

📋 What You Get Automatically

🎯 Focus on Writing

  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • No character or location management
  • Simple act and scene structure
  • Immediate access to AI writing help

🤖 AI Features Available

  • Writing Assistance: General help and suggestions
  • Story Continuation: "What happens next?" ideas
  • Dialogue Improvement: Better conversations
  • Scene Development: Expand your scenes
  • Plot Brainstorming: Story direction ideas
  • Writing Feedback: Improve your prose
  • AI Summaries: Auto-generate story/act/scene summaries

🏗️ Story Structure

Simple Hierarchy:
Basic Story → Acts → Scenes
Your first act is created automatically. Add more acts and scenes as needed.

📖 Acts and Scenes

  • Acts: Major story sections (chapters)
  • Scenes: Smaller sections within acts
  • No Limits: Create as many acts and scenes as you need
  • Simple Numbers: Act numbers only show when you have multiple acts

⚙️ Settings and Options

✏️ What You Can Customize

  • Story title and description
  • Act titles and content
  • Scene titles and content
  • Publishing settings

🚫 What's Not Available

  • Character profiles
  • Location details
  • World-building tools
  • Genre and tone settings
  • Complex metadata

📊 Templates and Starting Points

Template Options

Choose from these starting templates:

  • Blank Story: Empty story, complete creative freedom
  • Short Story: Structure for shorter narratives
  • Flash Fiction: Very short story format
  • Writing Exercise: Prompt-based writing practice

🔄 Migration and Upgrading

Important: You can upgrade a Basic Story to an Advanced Story at any time. This process reveals the hidden world and unlocks all advanced features. However, this upgrade cannot be undone.

When to Consider Upgrading:

  • Story becomes complex with multiple characters
  • You need location tracking
  • World-building becomes important
  • You want AI assistance with story context

💡 Tips for Success

Best Practices:
  • Keep it Simple: Focus on the story, not the tools
  • Use AI Help: Don't hesitate to ask for suggestions
  • Write First: Don't over-plan, just start writing
  • Regular Saves: Content is auto-saved as you type
  • Iterate: Write, get AI feedback, improve

🚨 Common Questions

Q: Can I add characters later?

A: Yes, by upgrading to an Advanced Story. Basic Stories focus on pure writing without character management.

Q: How many acts can I have?

A: Unlimited! Add as many acts and scenes as your story needs.

Q: Can I publish Basic Stories?

A: Absolutely! Basic Stories have full publishing capabilities just like Advanced Stories.

Q: What happens to my story if I upgrade?

A: Your content stays exactly the same. You gain access to character creation, locations, and advanced features.
