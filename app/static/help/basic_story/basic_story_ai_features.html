How to Use AI Assistance

1
Access AI Panel: Click the AI assistance button in your writing interface
2
Choose Assistance Type: Select from writing help, continuation, dialogue, etc.
3
Provide Context: Include your current text or specific questions
4
Review Suggestions: AI provides tailored suggestions for your content
5
Apply Selectively: Use suggestions that fit your vision and style

AI Best Practices

Do's

  • Use AI for inspiration and enhancement
  • Provide clear context in your requests
  • Iterate and refine with multiple requests
  • Maintain your unique writing voice
  • Use summaries for organization

Don'ts

  • Don't rely entirely on AI suggestions
  • Don't accept suggestions that don't fit
  • Don't expect AI to understand complex plots
  • Don't lose your creative vision
  • Don't forget to review and edit AI content
