Creating and Setting Up Stories

Create New Stories
Click "Create New Story" and provide basic information like title, description, and target audience. Choose a compelling title that reflects your story's essence.
Link to a World
Critical Step: Select one of your worlds from the dropdown. This connection enables the AI to use your world's context during writing for intelligent, consistent assistance.
Choose Story Class & Genre
Select the appropriate genre (Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Romance, etc.) and story class to help the AI understand the tone and style expectations for your narrative.
Set Up Initial Structure
Create your first Act with a descriptive name. You can add more acts and scenes as your story develops and grows in complexity.
💡 World Connection Tip
If you don't have a world yet, create one first using the World Builder Wizard or import from a book. Stories without world connections won't have access to intelligent AI context.

Story Structure & Organization

Acts and Scenes

Break your story into logical, manageable pieces for better organization and writing flow:

📖 Acts (Major Sections)
Large story divisions that represent major plot movements or time periods. Think of acts as chapters or major story beats.
  • Act 1: "The Discovery" - Introduction and setup
  • Act 2: "The Journey" - Main conflict development
  • Act 3: "The Resolution" - Climax and conclusion
🎬 Scenes (Specific Events)
Focused moments within acts that cover specific events, conversations, or locations. Scenes help maintain pacing and focus.
  • Scene 1: "Meeting at the Tavern"
  • Scene 2: "The Chase Through Town"
  • Scene 3: "Revelation in the Forest"
📝 Story Structure Best Practices
⏱️ Planning Time: 15-30 minutes
  1. 1
    Outline Before Writing: Plan your major story beats and key scenes before diving into detailed writing
  2. 2
    Use Descriptive Names: Name acts and scenes clearly so you can navigate easily later
  3. 3
    Keep Scenes Focused: Each scene should have a clear purpose and advance the plot or develop characters
  4. 4
    Link Key Characters: Connect important characters to your story for enhanced AI context and consistency

AI Writing Features

Once your story is linked to a world, you unlock powerful AI writing capabilities:

🎭 Contextual Character Writing
The AI knows your characters' personalities, speaking patterns, and relationships. Ask it to write dialogue or actions that are true to each character's nature.
🏰 Location-Aware Descriptions
When you mention locations from your world, the AI can provide rich, consistent descriptions that match your established world details.
📜 Lore Integration
The AI remembers your world's magic systems, history, and rules, ensuring that story elements remain consistent with your established lore.
✍️ Multiple Writing Modes
Use continue, expand, or rewrite modes depending on what you need. The AI adapts its approach based on your specific writing request.
🤖
AI Writing Best Practices
Be specific in your requests. Instead of "write more," try "write dialogue between John and Sarah where John reveals his secret" for much better, more targeted results.
