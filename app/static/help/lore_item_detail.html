Lore Item Management

Create rich lore items including artifacts, customs, laws, and historical events that add depth to your world.

Creating Lore Items:

  1. Choose item type (artifact, custom, law, event, etc.)
  2. Provide name and brief description
  3. Add historical significance and origins
  4. Define current status and location
  5. Use AI to expand on cultural implications

Key Features:

  • Artifact Creation: Magical items, weapons, and relics
  • Cultural Elements: Customs, traditions, and beliefs
  • Historical Events: Wars, discoveries, and turning points
  • Laws & Governance: Rules and political structures
  • AI Expansion: Generate detailed backgrounds and implications
Pro Tip: Connect lore items to your characters and locations. A legendary sword might be held by a character or hidden in a location.
Consistency Check: Ensure new lore items don't contradict existing world elements.
