# inkAndQuill Social Media Strategy & Templates

Complete social media strategy and content templates for inkAndQuill across 10 major platforms.

## Quick Access

**Common Templates:**
- [All Templates HTML](Templates/All_Social_Templates.html)
- [All Templates Text](Templates/All_Social_Templates.txt)

## Platform Structure

Each platform folder contains:
- **Platform Guide (.md)**: Comprehensive strategy, best practices, and task checklists
- **Welcome Templates**: HTML and text versions of welcome posts/content
- **Platform-Specific Assets**: Optimized for each network's requirements

### Platforms Included

1. **Instagram** - Visual storytelling focus with community building
2. **Twitter/X** - Real-time engagement and thought leadership  
3. **Facebook** - Community-focused with detailed content
4. **LinkedIn** - Professional networking and B2B outreach
5. **TikTok** - Short-form video content for younger demographics
6. **YouTube** - Long-form educational content and tutorials
7. **Pinterest** - Visual discovery and inspiration boards
8. **Discord** - Real-time community building and support
9. **Reddit** - Community participation and value-driven content
10. **Medium** - Thought leadership and in-depth articles
